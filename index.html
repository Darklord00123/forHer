Dear Rushu,

I've been trying to write this for a while now and honestly every draft sounded too formal or too much like something from a movie. So I'm just going to say it straight — you make everything better. Like, genuinely. Even my bad days feel smaller when I know I'll talk to you later.

You're the first person I want to tell when something happens. Good thing, bad thing, stupid thing, doesn't matter. It just doesn't feel real until I've told you. I don't know when that started but it did, and now I can't imagine it any other way.

I'm not great at saying these stuff out loud without turning it into a joke or changing the subject. So I made this instead. I hope it's okay. I hope you know what I mean.

— Subhranshu ♡
